About Us

Who We Are

Cinai IP engages with clients country-wide and across Sub-Saharan Africa. Our client base encompasses National Wireless and Fibre Operators, with successful engagements across a broad spectrum of complex infrastructure implementations.

Current Focus

Our focus is on the disciplines of providing the best in Technology and Communication Infrastructure. Cinai IP’s approach is to ensure that the correct discipline is effectively implemented, regardless of the technology used and product independent to ensure that the solutions provided are effective and aligned with the Business Objectives of our clients.

Experience

The Cinai IP team comprises Professionals with extensive experience in the Telecommunications sector specialising in Networks ranging from Copper, Fibre, IP, Satellite and Microwave technologies.

Customers must benefit tangibly through their dealings with Cinai IP. Openness and transparency through all transactions as we value long-term relationships.

Our Mission

To serve as a leading contract and resource provider, delivering customers with solutions designed to ensure that their contracts are managed optimally and business-critical infrastructure and environments are managed and operated at the lowest unit cost of operation.

Cinai IP’s focused approach, adaptability and vendor independence makes it the ideal choice for clients looking to optimise their existing environment, implement new or additional disciplines, or build multi-vendor management solutions.

Empowerment

Cinai IP subscribes to the philosophies and policies of Black Economic Empowerment, Employment Equity and Skills Development.

Cinai IP is committed to providing employment opportunities for previously disadvantaged individuals. We are committed to being a proudly South African company.
